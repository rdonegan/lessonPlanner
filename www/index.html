Help & Support

Tap a topic below to learn more. To close this panel, tap anywhere to the right.

  • Create a Lesson Plan

    Add a lesson plan by tapping "+" on the nav bar, or the action button at the bottom right of the home page. Tap a field in each section to edit. Some fields can only be edited if prior fields have been selected. Tap "Save" at the bottom to save the lesson plan.

    If no fields have been changed, the page will exit without saving.

  • View Lesson Plans

    The home screen displays all current lesson plans. By tapping "edit", you can scroll through all lesson plans previously created and currently stored.

    Tapping a lesson plan brings you to its edit page where you can view more details. By pressing the back button or "cancel" from this page, you can view lesson plan details without changing the plan.

  • Edit Lesson Plans

    To edit a lesson plan, simply tap on it on the homescreen or on the edit list page.

    From a lesson plan's edit page you can view all previously input information, as well as make any changes you like. Press "Save" to save your edits, or "Cancel" to return to the home screen without saving.

    On the edit page, fields are updated with prior selected standards, objectives, resources, etc. In some cases, you may need to check and uncheck one of these selected items to load applicable standards, objectives, resources, etc.

  • Delete Lesson Plans

    Deleting can be done from the edit list. Deleting is a permanent action and cannot be undone.

    To delete, swipe left on the item you wish to delete. This will reveal the delete option. Tap "delete" to remove from the list and database.

    In most cases, deleting is unnecessary. Instead, edit the lesson plan to reflect the changes you'd like to see.

  • Update Application

    To update the app with the most recent standards, objectives, resources, and performance indicators, tap the "Update App" button on the homescreen. Wait for the update process to complete.

    It is good to check for updates at least quarterly by tapping "Update App". This will ensure you're working from the most up-to-date information.

  • Share Lesson Plans

    Currently, you can share lesson plans by tapping "Share" on the upper right navigation bar.

    Follow the instructions on the Share popup: choose the range of lesson plans to export by selecting the desired time interval. Then, tap "Share".

    If sharing is successful, a file called "log.csv" will be saved on your device. You can access the file and transfer to USB, computer, or SD card by connecting your tablet to the desired device and transferring the file.

    Note that "log.csv" is overwritten each time you share.

    If you experience issues while trying to share your lesson plans, check that you've correctly filled in the desired "start date" and "end date" fields in the Share popup.

  • Errors and Troubleshooting

    It is possible that issues may occur while using the app. To help, it is recommended that you frequently share and save your lesson plans on another personal device in the event that the database is erased or you need to reinstall the app.

    Some recommended troubleshooting actions: close and restart the app, check your internet connection.

    If you experience problems after updating (e.g. you can't access standards and objectives when creating lesson plans), alert the app administrator to the issue. It is likely a database problem. Give them time to correct the issue, and then update the app again when instructed.
